About

Oluwanifise is also a Proofreader/Editor and a Voice Coach. She strongly believes that if anything is to be done at all, it deserves to be perfect. Speech, text, etc., need to be perfect and with skills such as attention to detail, patience and diligence, Oluwanifise edits all forms of text and trains public speakers to be more eloquent and confident in delivery of speech.
